Who We Are

MyCalendarTools is an independent Australian website built and maintained by Ascension Digital — a solo creator operation. We build date and calendar tools for the questions people search every day.

The Collaboration — The Owl & The Raven

These tools were built through an ongoing collaboration between human insight and artificial intelligence. That process has a symbolic framework we work within:

The Owl — perception, intuition, the ability to see patterns and meaning in what people actually need. The keeper of context.

The Raven — structure, precision, turning ideas into systems, code, and tools that actually work. The builder.

Between them is the space where thought becomes something real. Not just code. Not just ideas. The intersection of both. That is where these tools come from.
