About MyCalendarTools

Free date tools, calendars, countdowns and time utilities.
No accounts. No noise. Just answers.

What Is MyCalendarTools?

MyCalendarTools is a free, Australian-built website offering a comprehensive suite of date, calendar and time tools for everyday use. Whether you need to calculate how many days until a holiday, find out what day of the week a past date fell on, look up moon phases, check school holiday dates, or convert time zones for an international call — there is a tool here for it.

Every tool is designed to work instantly in your browser with no sign-up, no subscription and no personal data collection. You get an answer immediately, without friction. The site covers more than 24 tools across date calculation, calendar planning, countdowns, astronomy, school terms and time management.

Our Tools

MyCalendarTools includes tools in several categories:

  • Date calculators — days between dates, weeks between, months between, date add/subtract, age calculator, day of the week, week number
  • Countdowns — custom countdowns, birthday countdowns, days until Christmas, New Year, Easter and more
  • Calendar & planning — printable calendar, year at a glance, school holidays, school term dates, next public holiday
  • Astronomy — daily moon phase, full moon calendar, zodiac signs
  • Time tools — world clock, time zone converter, stopwatch, countdown timer, date reminders
  • Coastal tools — NOAA tide times for US coastal stations
