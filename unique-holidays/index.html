About this calendar

Discover weird and wonderful unofficial holidays throughout the year. It runs entirely in your browser, updates live, and needs no sign-up — just a quick way to see how long is left.

How to use Unique & Unusual Holidays

  1. Pick the date or event you're counting down to (or from).
  2. The countdown updates live — no need to refresh the page.
  3. Bookmark the page if you want to check back closer to the date.
  4. Share the link if you want others to see the same countdown.

When this page is useful

Unique & Unusual Holidays is commonly used for: Building anticipation for an upcoming event or milestone. Planning how much time is left to prepare for something.

The result should be treated as a practical guide rather than professional advice. For legal, financial, health, tax, pregnancy, school holiday, tide or public holiday decisions, confirm important details with an official source or qualified professional.
