About Date Reminders

The MyCalendarTools Date Reminders tool lets you create a personal list of upcoming dates and deadlines, stored entirely in your browser. No account needed, no data sent to a server — everything lives in your device's local storage. This means your reminders are private, instant to set up, and always available as long as you return to this page on the same browser.

Reminders are checked automatically every 30 seconds while the page is open. When a reminder date and time is reached, you'll see a browser alert with the reminder title. For the most reliable experience, keep this tab open in your browser — especially for time-sensitive reminders.

How to set a reminder

  1. Type a title that describes what you need to remember (e.g., "Pay phone bill", "Submit assignment").
  2. Select the date and time you want to be reminded.
  3. Click Save Reminder — it appears in your saved list immediately.
  4. Reminders are sorted by date, so the nearest one always appears at the top.
  5. Click Remove on any reminder once it's done.

Who uses date reminders?

  • Students — track assignment submission dates and exam days.
  • Professionals — keep an eye on billing cycles, contract renewals, and follow-ups.
  • Parents — remember school events, excursion payments, and permission slips.
  • Anyone planning ahead — birthdays, anniversaries, appointments, and subscription renewals.

🔒 Privacy note: Reminders are saved using your browser's local storage and remain on this device only. They are not synced to any account or shared with our servers. Clearing your browser data will remove saved reminders.
