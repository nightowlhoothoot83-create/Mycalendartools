Frequently Asked Questions

Diwali falls on the 15th day of Kartik, the holiest month of the Hindu lunisolar calendar. It typically occurs between October and November. In 2026 Diwali falls on November 8.

Diwali is a five-day festival. The main celebration — the Festival of Lights — occurs on the third day.

Diwali celebrates the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. It is observed by Hindus, Jains, Sikhs, and some Buddhists around the world.
