🌍 Global Coverage

Tide data covers all major ocean coastlines worldwide — including Australia, New Zealand, UK, US, Pacific and Indian Ocean regions.

📊 What the data shows

Sea surface height above mean sea level in metres. Positive values = above average, negative = below. Higher values indicate high tide conditions; lower values indicate low tide.

⚠️ Safety note

This data is for general guidance only. Always check official local tide tables before swimming, boating or coastal crossings. Conditions can vary due to storms and local factors.

About This Tide Tool

This tool uses the Norwegian Meteorological Institute's ocean forecast model (Havvarsel-Møre 800m) to provide sea surface height predictions for coastal locations worldwide. The model is updated every six hours and provides hourly forecasts for approximately 48 hours ahead.

Location search uses OpenStreetMap Nominatim to convert place names into coordinates, then passes those coordinates to the ocean forecast API. Your location is only used for this look-up and nothing is stored.
